📖 How to Get Started

  1. Click "Start Session" - Choose your profile and configure
  2. Watch the Log - Events will appear in the right panel
  3. Use the Sliders - Adjust your current physical state when prompted

  5. Acknowledge or Skip - When the alarm banner appears, choose your action
